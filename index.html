About Raychelle

Raychelle is an adaptable writer, yogi, garderner and beekeeper. Born and raised in Texas, she made her way to Austin from her hometown of Houston several years ago. She has applied her writing expertise across numerous sectors. When she isn't working, Raychelle enjoys practicing yoga, regenerative gardening, and helping to save the endangered bees of Central Texas.

Raychelle holds a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism from the University of Houston. She is currently working toward a Master of Arts in Technical Communication to expand her communications skillset.
